About

medupi is a rehearsal of freedom, a house of collective study, a taking in of breath into bodies - celestial and more. These are small exercises and practices of abundance. medupi is an invitation to be fearlessly free. The practice is in book and thought formations energised by the many worlds we inhabit. Kabelo Malatsie founded medupi after working on books for many years in various capacities; as editor, distributor, and publisher.
